Erwtensoep

Ingredients

  • Split peas
  • Water
  • Potatoes
  • Carrots
  • Shoulder chops
  • Leek
  • Onion

Instructions

  1. Let the water cook with stock cubes, split peas and shoulder chops
  2. Chop the carrots, onion and potatoes
  3. After an hour steer the soup
  4. Pick the shoulder chops and cut it in pieces and put it back
  5. Let it cook for 30 minutes and enjoy!
